faq

Can BatchWatermark Image watermark full folders at once?

Yes. It is designed for full-folder batch processing so you can configure the watermark once and apply it across the selected image set.

Yes. You can use an image watermark, a text watermark, or both together in the same output batch.

Yes. The tiling and rotation controls make it suitable for proof overlays, not just a single corner watermark.

It works especially well for photographers, ecommerce sellers, print-preview workflows, and anyone who needs repeatable proof or branding overlays.

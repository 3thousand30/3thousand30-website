privacy_and_control

This is a bring-your-own-key image workflow. You choose the provider, model, and cost profile. API keys are encrypted locally on your machine. The exported images are saved to your PC.

Important distinction: the saved outputs stay on your machine, but the prompt request goes to the external image provider you selected. That is a controlled tradeoff, not a hidden cloud layer from us.

faq

What kinds of images can BatchGen Image with AI generate?

It is suited to social visuals, posters, mockups, book covers, photobook images, launch graphics, banners, and other batch-style image workflows.

Can I choose my own provider and model?

Yes. This is a bring-your-own-key workflow with provider presets and custom endpoints, so you choose the model and pay the provider directly.

Do the files stay on my machine?

Yes. Generated files are saved locally on your machine. If you use an external AI provider, the prompt request goes to that provider.

Is it good for posters and photobooks?

Yes. It is one of the strongest fits for posters, photobook concepts, print-led image batches, and repeatable visual exploration before print prep.

Do I still need to review the images?

Yes. Human selection still matters. You should reject weak anatomy, weak composition, and off-theme results rather than assuming every batch output is ready.
